The Envoy Hotel


For your convenience, a discounted rate is available at the Envoy Hotel, just a 5 minute walk from the Institute of Contemporary Art.


Book on their website: www.theenvoyhotel.com


Boston Logan International Airport (BOS)


Guests traveling by air should plan to fly in to Logan Airport (Airport Code: BOS). From there, subway users can take the Silver Line to the "Court House" stop, which is a 2 minute walk from the Envoy Hotel.
